Introduction

Dr. Muhammad Abdur Rehman is a neurosurgeon with over nine years of experience practicing at Mujahid Hospital in Faisalabad, Pakistan. He specializes in treating conditions related to the brain, spine, and nervous system.

Dr. Rehman is known for his expertise in performing various surgical procedures, including those for meningocele, meningomyelocele, hydrocephalus, brain tumors, head injuries, and spinal tumors. He is also skilled in providing non-surgical treatments for conditions such as back pain and headaches.
